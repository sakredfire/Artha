📊 What Indian Banks Accept
✅ Rental Income
Accepted by all banks with registered rent agreement + 3–6 months bank statement showing credits.
✅ Freelance / Gig Income
Accepted with 12-month ITR + bank statement. HDFC, ICICI, Kotak are most freelancer-friendly.
⚠️ Cash Income
Not accepted without bank proof. Deposit consistently and get a CA letter for best results.
