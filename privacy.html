🔒 The Short Version: Artha stores all your data on your device. We never sell your data or share it with advertisers. No account is required. The only time data leaves your device is when you use Stacks AI — then a brief summary of your figures is sent to our AI provider (Anthropic) to generate a reply, and is not stored afterwards.

Data Deletion

Artha stores your income, expense, mood, and goal data only on your device — we keep no copy on any server.

To delete your data: open Artha and clear the app's data, or uninstall the app. Either action permanently and immediately deletes all locally stored data. Because it lives only on your device, no server-side copy exists.

Stacks AI: when you ask a question, your question and a brief summary of your figures are sent to our AI provider only to generate a reply, and are not stored by us afterwards.

To request deletion or raise a privacy concern: email mihir86.mp@gmail.com — we action verified requests within 7 days.

4. Where Data Is Stored

All user data is stored exclusively in your device's browser localStorage / IndexedDB. Your stored data leaves the device only when (a) you use the optional "Export My Data" feature (saves a JSON file to your own device), or (b) you use Stacks AI, which sends a brief summary of your figures to our AI provider to generate a reply (not stored afterwards — see §6). If you uninstall the app or clear browser data, all Artha data is permanently deleted.

Cloud backup is an opt-in feature only. It is disabled by default. If enabled by the user in Settings, data is encrypted before any transmission. This feature is not yet live in v5.0.

8. Your Rights Under the DPDP Act 2023

Under India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023, you have the following rights:

RightHow to Exercise
Right to Access — know what data we holdAll data is in your device localStorage. Settings → Export My Data (JSON)
Right to Correction — correct inaccurate dataEdit directly in the app (Profile, Income Log, etc.)
Right to Erasure — delete your dataSettings → Delete Account (clears all local data instantly)
Right to Data Portability — receive your dataSettings → Export My Data → downloads JSON file
Right to Withdraw ConsentRevoke permissions in device settings at any time
Right to Grievance RedressalEmail mihir86.mp@gmail.com — response within 72 hours

Your records are stored on your device, so exercising most rights is immediate and does not require contacting us. When you use AI features, the relevant text is sent to our secure AI provider (Cloudflare Worker → Anthropic) as described in §6 — encrypted in transit and never sold.

9. Data Retention

Data persists on your device until you:

  • Use Settings → Delete Account (immediate, permanent)
  • Clear your browser's site data / app storage
  • Uninstall the app

We do not retain copies of your data on any server. There is no backup to delete from our side.

10. Security

  • App PIN is stored as base64 encoding (obfuscated, not plaintext)
  • Biometric authentication uses WebAuthn — actual biometric data is never accessible to the app
  • All connections use HTTPS (TLS 1.2+)
  • Permissions-Policy headers restrict camera and microphone to the app origin only
  • Content Security Policy prevents injection of unauthorized scripts

In the event of a security incident affecting user data, we will notify affected users within 72 hours as required by DPDP 2023.
